Welcome to Mainstreet Connections!

Main Street Connections is a local telephone business located in Jackson TN, but don’t let the title telephone business fool you, we do so much more!

At Mainstreet Connections, we provide you with the best services possible. We can meet any of your telecommunication needs. We can install phone lines, cameras , security systems and more! We will complete the job and leave the worksite just as neat as we left it. Our highly certified technicians will do their job the most efficient way possible. We will not disturb your normal work day in the proccess of completing the order.

Our knowledgeable and courteous staff are here to assist you and your business grow and become even more manageable during your growth or start-up. Our staff will take the time to listen to your business objectives and help guide you to the telephone system best suited for your Telecommunication needs. The improvement of your business's productivity is simply one call away. MSC is dedicated to providing prompt and reliable service to any business need and will make sure that your objectives are followed and the task is completed with as little disruption as possible.

At mainstreet Connections, we realize our most valuable asset is you, the customer! Thank you for your time and we hope to hear from you in the near future!
